Strategic Initiative

Because of a pharmacist, something important happened.

Behind The Counter is a collective archive of pharmacy impact — a space where the everyday work of pharmacists is made visible through real stories.

Why it exists

To document the often unseen clinical and non-clinical decisions pharmacists make every day — decisions that shape care, access, safety, and outcomes.

Every day, pharmacists make decisions that shape patient outcomes.

Some are clearly clinical — identifying a dosing issue, preventing a drug interaction, or guiding therapy. Others are less visible — navigating insurance barriers, coordinating care, resolving access challenges, and managing complex systems behind the scenes.

These moments, both clinical and non-clinical, form the full scope of pharmacy practice — work that is essential, continuous, and often unseen.

Yet many of these contributions are never formally documented in a way that captures their depth, context, and impact. They happen quietly, in real time, and are often carried forward only in the memory of the pharmacist.

Behind The Counter exists to change that.

What the platform does

A simple submission process built around authenticity, clarity, and confidentiality.

01

Capture the moment

Pharmacists submit brief notes — however incomplete or unstructured — describing a moment that stood out in practice.

02

Refine the story

Those notes are shaped into clear, professional narratives that preserve the contributor’s intent while removing identifying details.

03

Review before publication

Contributors review and confirm each story before it enters editorial review and publication to the archive.

The result is a growing archive of authentic, anonymized stories that reflect the real-world impact of pharmacy practice.

Why it matters

Behind The Counter reflects the kind of systems-level thinking that we bring to healthcare challenges.

The initiative was developed in response to an observed gap in how pharmacy impact is captured, communicated and understood - demonstrating our broader approach to identifying unmet needs, designing practical solutions and advancing innovation within heaalthcare systems.

Much of this work goes unseen. When it is unseen, it is often undervalued or misunderstood.
