About

A consulting firm grounded in healthcare delivery, leadership, and system improvement.

Healthy Hills Consulting Inc. provides healthcare advisory and operational consulting services with a focus on rural, remote, and Indigenous community settings. Our work is shaped by direct leadership experience, committee service, and a practical commitment to improving access and quality.

Leadership

Healthy Hills Consulting Inc. is led by Augustine Ezugwu, a pharmacist and healthcare leader with experience managing clinical and operational services in a rural and remote pharmacy serving an Indigenous community in Northern Alberta.

His background includes contributions to Alberta’s Expert Committee on Drug Evaluation and Therapeutics, the Competence Committee of the Alberta College of Pharmacy, and national clinical education initiatives, including content review for the SOGC CAPS program.
